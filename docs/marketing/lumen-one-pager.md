# Lumen — One-pager
**Program & project intelligence platform for multi-country organizations**

**Lumen** helps humanitarian and development networks manage complexity across **organization → programs → projects** with one shared source of truth. It is designed for teams working with multiple donors, multiple partners, and strong accountability requirements.

---

## The problem
In many organizations, portfolio visibility is fragmented across spreadsheets, emails, and scattered documents. That fragmentation creates recurring pain:

- Slow decision-making because information is hard to consolidate.
- Financial blind spots (burn rate surprises, delayed reporting, unclear budget vs actual).
- Limited operational visibility across regions, partners, and workstreams.
- Risks and compliance tracked inconsistently, making governance harder.

---

## What Lumen provides
Lumen brings portfolio-level clarity without losing project-level detail.

### Executive visibility
- **Executive dashboard**: active projects, budget totals, utilization, beneficiaries, projects ending soon, and high-risk alerts.

### Program performance
- **Program dashboards**: KPIs per thematic area, donor breakdown, risk summary, and project lists.

### Project accountability
- **Project detail view**: general information, financials, implementation status, M&E indicators, budget lines, risk register, and documents.

### Financial oversight
- Budget vs actual tracking and burn-rate monitoring.
- Spending alerts (slow/fast) and improved readiness for donor reporting.

### Geographic overview
- Portfolio view by region and location tables for multi-site delivery.

### Risk & compliance
- Risk register and compliance checklist to support governance and data security.

### Roles and collaboration
Designed for cross-functional teams, including:
Executive Director, Program Manager, Project Manager, Finance Officer, M&E Officer, and Partner/Diocese users.

---

## Who it is for
Lumen is built for organizations that:

- Run multiple programs and projects in parallel (often across multiple countries).
- Manage several donors and partners with strict reporting needs.
- Need a shared dashboard for leadership **and** working tools for program/finance/M&E teams.

---

## Outcomes you can expect
- Faster portfolio decisions with fewer manual consolidations.
- Stronger financial and operational control through shared, up-to-date information.
- Better accountability with consistent project documentation, indicators, and risk tracking.

---

## Platform notes (for IT / operations)
- Built with **Next.js**, **React**, **TypeScript**, and a **MongoDB** data layer.
- A new production environment starts **empty** (no seeded demo accounts/data). Users create their organization and add programs/projects.
